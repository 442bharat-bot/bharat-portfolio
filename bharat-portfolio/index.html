Predictive Modeling & CRM Integration

Customer Churn Prediction for Credit Cards

The Problem: Banks struggle to identify subtle behavioral shifts that indicate a customer is preparing to cancel their card. Acquiring a new user costs 5x more than retaining one, making early churn prediction a massive commercial priority.

The Architecture: Engineered a robust predictive workflow utilizing demographic, transactional, and engagement data. Processed missing values, addressed class imbalance (SMOTE), and trained an ensemble model optimized for Recall.

Solution Design: Built an automated pipeline that continuously scores the customer base. "High Risk" profiles are automatically routed via API into the retention team's CRM alongside their top churn drivers (e.g., sudden drop in utilization).
